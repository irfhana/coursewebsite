About Us

Meet Mr. Arun

With over three decades of rich experience in the field of journalism, Mr. Arun has witnessed history unfold before his eyes. From covering ground-breaking stories to interviewing influential personalities, his career has been nothing short of extraordinary. Now retired, Mr. Arun is passionate about sharing his invaluable insights and expertise with the next generation of journalists.
